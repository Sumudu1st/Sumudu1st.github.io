House Plan Designs

The desire of many people is certainly to be the owner of a home. As a result, creating a family nest for your loved ones might be your life's biggest investment. Being aware of the availability of specific residential building design codes, high-tech materials, new construction methods, to name a few, the consumers will naturally demand a high standard of design for their future home. For this reason, today's consumer tends to rely on specialists for the design of his dream home, and Susantha Builders will assure you such designs.

All floor plans, reference drawings, architectural and engineering plans, are submitted to a rigorous inspection program which is continuously updated. This inspection program was elaborated from knowledge gained over the years and from specific code requirements of Sri Lanka.

Interior Designing

We know that the home is an outer reflection of our personalities and we take care to reflect that individuality with every one of our clients.We work with you on space planning, creation of colour and decorating schemes, we source the best and most appropriate furnishings and fabrics, produce budget plans, recommend contractors and work with them to produce a schedule of works for your project.

Our first consultation with you is always free and we visit your home to discuss your ideas and design preferences for your new scheme. From that meeting we will prepare a list of tasks and actions we anticipate will be needed to complete your project together with a budget estimate for our consultation fees.
