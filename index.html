About

I am an experienced freelance graphic designer for local and remote clients who has a BTec Mechatronics Engineering degree specializing in machine vision.

Also, I have satisfied clients with flyer design, label design, and web design.

I am also certified in Python, project management, Human resource management, and HTML5.

Designing is my hobby and I am in freelancing ever since I am an undergraduate. I try my best to give a quality output to my clients because the satisfaction of the client is what makes me happy.

I always try to satisfy my remote clients as my local clients.
